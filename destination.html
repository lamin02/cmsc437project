Rome Destination Guide

Colosseum

The iconic symbol of Rome, the Colosseum is an ancient amphitheater that once hosted gladiator battles and public spectacles. Explore its storied ruins and imagine the roar of the crowds!

Trevi Fountain

Toss a coin and make a wish at the stunning Trevi Fountain, a Baroque masterpiece and one of the most famous fountains in the world.

Pantheon

This remarkably preserved temple, now a church, boasts a massive domed roof and oculus. Step inside to experience the grandeur of ancient Roman engineering.

Vatican City & St. Peter's Basilica

Visit the heart of Catholicism and marvel at the art and architecture of St. Peter's Basilica. Don't miss the Vatican Museums and the Sistine Chapel, home to Michelangelo's famous ceiling.

Piazza Navona

A lively square filled with street artists, fountains, and cafes. Enjoy the vibrant atmosphere and admire Bernini's Fountain of the Four Rivers.

Trastevere

Wander the charming cobblestone streets of Trastevere, a neighborhood known for its authentic Roman cuisine, nightlife, and picturesque alleys.

Roman Forum

Walk among the ruins of temples, basilicas, and public spaces that were once the center of ancient Roman life and politics.

Spanish Steps

Climb the famous staircase and enjoy people-watching at this popular gathering spot, surrounded by shops and cafes.
